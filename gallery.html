apreciando suas crenças / Lisboa, Portugal / 2023

All paintings are printed by hand on exquisite Hahnemuhle paper made of 100% cotton.
The paintings printed on this paper look very beautiful, but are fragile, so the standard form of delivery is canvas packed in a plastic tube.

On request, the painting can be mounted in a glass frame, you can see examples here.
