apreciando suas crenças / Lisboa, Portugal / 2023

Welcome to the world of my photographic paintings. I am a photographer from Portugal.

I draw my inspiration from my beloved Algarve house, which is located in the south of Portugal.
Nature has endowed our region with an incredible palette of colors, and the locals have decorated this region with incredibly beautiful architecture.
